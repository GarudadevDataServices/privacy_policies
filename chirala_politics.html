Chirala Politics App Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which Chirala Politics app collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users.

The Chirala Politics app does not collect any personal data from users. However, usage logs are collected via an API for the purpose of analyzing user behavior and improving the app.

The collected usage log data is solely used for analyzing user behavior and improving the app. It is not used directly within the app, and no data is shared with third parties.

The usage log data collected is protected and stored securely.

There are no age restrictions for users of the Chirala Politics app.

Cookies
